# EV0L — STATUS & Hedge Log

Purpose
- Rolling operational log for hedges, drills, and Sabbath activations.
- Human-friendly place to mark weekly activations, drill outcomes, and proof artifacts.

Template
- Week: YYYY-WW
- Hedges active: [1,2,3,...]
- Sabbath: on/off (timestamp)
- Drill activations: list of drills & short results
- Notes / evidence: links to deployment files, tx hashes, screenshots

Example
- Week: 2025-W46
- Hedges active: 1–12
- Sabbath: on (2025-11-09T18:00:00Z)
- Drill activations:
  - City posture test (90s posture) — PASS
  - Ledger freeze vault test — PASS
- Notes:
  - deployments/mumbai.json @ commit <sha>
  - Pulse Archive CID: ipfs://bafy...
